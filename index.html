Social Entrepreneurship

"Empowering Threads" is a transformative initiative that empowers underprivileged women in Jabalpur by fostering entrepreneurship in the Indian ethnic wear industry. It collects surplus fashion inventory, refurbishes and upgrades the designs, and then empowers women to manage their small-scale boutiques, providing them with financial independence and entrepreneurial skills. This innovative approach not only reduces fashion waste but also creates economic opportunities, bringing positive change to the lives of women in our community.
